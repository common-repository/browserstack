Generate screenshots of your Wordpress page across multiple mobile and desktop browsers in a click.

== Description ==
Generate screenshots of your Wordpress page across multiple mobile and desktop browsers in a single click. Debug instantly by starting a Live session from the screenshot dashboard.

### Why should you use this plugin?
1. Test your web pages instantly by generating screenshots. 
1. Ensure uniform rendering across all device-browser combinations.
1. Debug instantly by starting a BrowserStack Live session from the screenshot dashboard.

### Steps to use:

1. Install the plugin, and enable it after the installation is complete.
1. Open the WordPress dashboard, hover over the page/post you want to take a screenshot of.
1. Click on the 1-click screenshot button to generate screenshots of your page/post.

Detailed documentation available [here](https://www.browserstack.com/wordpress-integration).

== Installation ==

Once you have installed the plugin and [created your account](https://www.browserstack.com/users/sign_up?utm_source=wordpress-plugin-page&utm_medium=wordpress&utm_campaign=FreeTrial_Promotional&utm_content=wordpress_users&utm_term=sign_up), you can start taking screenshots for your pages and posts. You can take screenshots from the preview section of your page and posts as well.
